About

Our honey is 100% pure, and we have been producing it for over 30 years. We thoughtfully select bees that can adapt to the harsh winters and short growing season typical of our home at the base of the Big Horn Mountains.

As a result, our prolific pollinators have evolved to thrive in our local ecosystem, foraging on everything from sweet clover to wildflowers and even linden trees. Our honey is a true taste of Wyoming, and every year is a little different.
